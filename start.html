What's inside

Three places the problem is usually hiding.

01

Start getting out of the way

Most business owners are either a bottleneck, or carry responsibilities no one else knows how to do. Learn how to start handing them off, and what you learn when you try.

02

Get your key numbers on one page

Find the most critical numbers that show the health of your business and get them on one page. If you can't find the numbers, you have your first problem to solve.

03

Get honest about your top customers

Most businesses make most of their profit from a small percentage of their customers, but they aren't always the ones you think. Take a more honest read of where your money is coming from.
