What We Offer

Three ways we work
with your business

Each service is available as a standalone engagement or as part of a broader management consulting relationship.

Strategic Planning & Implementation

A structured annual and quarterly planning program that aligns your leadership team, defines clear priorities, and builds the accountability rhythm to execute on them.

  • Annual 2-day strategic planning session
  • 3 quarterly planning days
  • Weekly leadership meeting facilitation
  • Monthly performance review meetings
  • Regular coaching for owner and key leaders
  • Full Year 1 implementation and onboarding

Business Coaching

One-on-one coaching for business owners and senior leaders navigating growth, transition, or the complexity of running a business. Practical, direct, and grounded in real operational experience.

  • Structured sessions tailored to your cadence
  • Goal-setting and accountability frameworks
  • Decision-making and leadership development
  • Business performance and financial clarity
  • Exit readiness and owner transition planning
  • Buy or sell-side transaction preparation

Fractional IT Leadership

CIO-level technology leadership for businesses that need senior IT strategy and oversight without the full-time headcount. Bridge the gap between your operational IT team and the boardroom.

  • IT strategy and multi-year roadmap development
  • Technology investment planning and prioritization
  • Vendor and MSP selection, negotiation and oversight
  • IT governance, policy and risk management
  • Leadership team technology education
  • Project oversight and delivery accountability
